# 🏷️ Labels Used

The workflow classifies Gmail messages into one of the following labels:

| Label | Description |
|---|---|
| Bills & Utilities | Bills, utilities, payment notices, invoices, rent, phone, internet, and related receipts |
| Alerts & Newsletters | Automated alerts, promotional newsletters, marketing emails, and subscription digests |
| Shipping & Orders | Online purchases, order confirmations, delivery updates, package tracking, and shipment status |
| Learning & Courses | Course updates, training emails, certificates, and learning platform notifications |
| High Priority | Emails requiring immediate action from the user, especially with deadlines or consequences |
| Job Alerts | Job openings, recruiter alerts, interview updates, application status, and career platform emails |
